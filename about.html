Santa Rosa National High School has gone far and the significantly improve from its humed beginnings it was initially build beside the Santa Rosa Central School using sawali in classrooms. From this former name Sto . Rosario High School – Annex it was rename Santa Rosa National High School in the year 2000.

In Year 2004, It was transferred in Brgy. Soledad, Santa Rosa, Nueva Ecija in the middle of mass green field with fresh air in the majestic scenery. The school site was donated by Dona Eugena an Heir of Puyat Clan and who son of Mr. Alfonso Puyat gave an additional 4000 sq meters to Santa Rosa National High School as an expansion of its area.

From dilapidated sawali classrooms that were usually submerge in flood water during rainy season from its former site. It was transform to a two story and twelve classrooms concreated building after sometime another one story twelve classrooms adjacent the side fences of the school was constructed that gradual start was efforts of former Governor Tomas N. Joson III and Mayor of Santa Rosa Josefino M. Angeles through the years of existence and outstanding performance of those principals who handled the school many significant changes have transpired in the physical facilities of SRNHS to make it conducive place for the learners.

More buildings were built like the DepEd buiding in the front of gymnasium two more buildings at the back of it, the Techvoc and the Spa building. One story two classroom DepEd building at the back of Spa two story building beside the gymnasium and another four story building erected at additional lotted back of Grade 7 building.

The SRNHS has been lucky to have stakeholders who have been giving allouts support like Mr. Alfonso Puyat who gave additional lot for the school were the four story building was later constructed. Moreover, the gymnasium was given by Governor Aurelio Oyie Umali while the stage erected through the generousity of Congressman Joseph Gilbert Violago.

The parents, the teachers and their students worked hand in hand in making the school wonderful place for every learner. On the other hand, to keep abreast with the modern world of technology. Because of the school's stunning beauty, the SRNHS gained its Hall of Famer title in Brigada Eskwela, a competition for the cleanest and the most beautiful school in the entire Philippines. It was achieved during the realm of Rowena B. Caoile and another moment was during the time of Hilaria E. The SRNHS has been steadfast in soaring to the horizon of excellence. Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where: STUDENTS learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. TEACHERS facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. ADMINISTRATORS and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen. FAMILY, COMMUNITY, and other STAKEHOLDERS are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learner.

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.
