Flag Raising Ceremony

The flag ceremony at Santa Rosa National High School in Nueva Ecija is a solemn and dignified event that reflects the school's respect for national symbols and values. Held regularly, the ceremony brings together students, teachers, and staff to honor the Philippine flag and reaffirm their commitment to the nation.

Founding Anniversary

Santa Rosa National High School in Nueva Ecija celebrates its founding anniversary with great enthusiasm and pride. The event typically includes a series of activities such as parades, cultural performances, sports competitions, and academic showcases. Alumni, current students, faculty, and staff come together to commemorate the school's history and achievements. There are often speeches highlighting the school's milestones and contributions to the community, as well as recognition of outstanding individuals who have made significant contributions to the school's success. The founding anniversary is a time for reflection, gratitude, and celebration of the school's legacy and impact on generations of students.

Chalk Art Competetion

Students from Santa Rosa National High School in Nueva Ecija participate in creative expression activities where they create works of art using chalk on the school's cement flooring. These gatherings frequently highlight a variety of topics, such as cultural festivals and environmental awareness initiatives. With great enthusiasm, the students convert the lifeless cement into colourful paintings, converting the school grounds into bright, energetic areas. Along with being decorations, the chalk drawings allow children to express themselves and participate with the community, which builds pride and a sense of community among them.

Prom Unmask Night

The JS (Junior-Senior) Prom Unmask Night at Santa Rosa National High School in Nueva Ecija is a highly anticipated event filled with excitement and elegance. Students dress in formal attire and masks, adding an air of mystery and sophistication to the evening. The event typically features a grand entrance, where students unveil their masks, revealing their identities to one another. There are usually dinner and dancing, creating a memorable experience for everyone in attendance. The JS Prom Unmask Night provides an opportunity for students to socialize, make lasting memories, and celebrate the culmination of their high school years in style.

Art Exhibit

Santa Rosa National High School in Nueva Ecija hosts the "Likha Liwan Art Exhibit," a dynamic program that celebrates visual arts and creative writing in media arts. This exhibit serves as a platform for students to showcase their talents and express themselves through various artistic mediums. From captivating paintings and sculptures to thought-provoking multimedia installations, the exhibit offers a diverse range of artistic expressions.

SPA Recital

The annual recital of the Special Program for the Arts at Santa Rosa National High School is a vibrant celebration of talent, creativity, and dedication. Each year, students showcase their skills in various artistic disciplines, captivating audiences with their performances in music, dance, theater, and visual arts. The recital not only provides a platform for students to share their passion for the arts but also fosters a sense of community and pride within the school. It serves as a testament to the importance of arts education in nurturing well-rounded individuals and preparing them for future success.

Commencement Exercise

Santa Rosa National High School's commencement exercise is a momentous occasion that embodies the culmination of years of dedication, hard work, and academic achievement. Held with great anticipation and excitement, this event symbolizes the transition of graduating students from their high school journey to the next chapter of their lives. Set against the backdrop of the school's picturesque campus, adorned with vibrant decorations and fluttering banners, the commencement ceremony radiates an atmosphere of pride, joy, and celebration. Faculty members, parents, families, and friends gather in the school's grand auditorium, eagerly awaiting the commencement address, heartfelt speeches, and the presentation of diplomas. As each graduate crosses the stage, their accomplishments are celebrated with thunderous applause and cheers of support from their loved ones.

Baccalaureate Mass

The Baccalaureate Mass at Santa Rosa National High School is a cherished tradition where graduating students, families, and faculty gather for a solemn yet joyous ceremony. Held in the school's chapel or assembly hall, it's a time for prayer, reflection, and seeking divine blessings for the graduates' future endeavors. With uplifting hymns and a heartfelt homily, the Mass symbolizes spiritual unity and gratitude, preparing students for the journey ahead with faith and resilience.

SPA-Music Performance on CLRAA 2023

The Governor's Night at Santa Rosa National High School in Nueva Ecija transforms the campus into a vibrant celebration of sportsmanship and cultural richness. Athletes, coaches, and guests gather for an evening of recognition, dynamic performances, and awards honoring the achievements of participants in the CLRAA competitions. The event features captivating cultural displays, formal ceremonies, and a festive atmosphere, fostering camaraderie and unity among attendees.

Poster Making Contest

The Poster Making Contest at Santa Rosa National High School in Nueva Ecija is a platform for students to showcase their artistic skills and convey important messages through visually captivating posters. Participants from different grades create artworks on themes like environmental conservation or cultural diversity, guided by teachers and staff. The contest culminates in an awards ceremony where winners are celebrated, but all participants gain valuable experience and contribute to meaningful dialogue.
